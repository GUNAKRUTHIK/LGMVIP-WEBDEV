Top china gamer


Chinese people are obsessed with games. In fact, nearly 560 million people, seven out of 10 of the country’s online population, claim to play games. So it should come as no surprise that the eSports industry — where professional gamers compete against other teams as spectators watch — is extremely popular in China. Over the past ten years China has seen the number of professional gamers grow from only 50 to over 1,000.
